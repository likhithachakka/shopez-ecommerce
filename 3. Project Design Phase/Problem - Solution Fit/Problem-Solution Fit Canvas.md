## Purpose

The Problem-Solution Fit validates that ShopEZ solves a genuine customer problem — online shoppers overwhelmed by cluttered, slow e-commerce platforms — and that the solution fits naturally into existing user behaviours. This canvas maps the customer segment, their constraints, available alternatives, triggers, emotions, behavioural channels, root cause, and finally our solution.

---

## 1. Customer Segment(s)

**Who is your customer?**

**Primary:** Urban and suburban online shoppers (ages 18–45) who value convenience, curated discovery, and frictionless checkout. They are tech-comfortable but time-constrained — professionals, college students, and busy parents.

**Secondary:** Small business owners / independent sellers who want a simple platform to list and sell curated goods without technical overhead or high commissions.

**Which jobs-to-be-done (or problems) do you address for your customers?**

| # | Job / Problem | Description |
|---|---|---|

| JP-2 | **Frictionless checkout** | Complete a purchase in minimal steps without forced account creation |
| JP-3 | **Trustworthy shopping** | Transparent pricing with clear discount visibility and no hidden costs |
| JP-4 | **Order confidence** | Know the order status with confirmation and history tracking |

## 5. Available Solutions

**Which solutions are available to customers when they face the problem?**

| # | Existing Solution | Pros | Cons |
|---|---|---|---|
| AS-1 | Amazon / Flipkart | Massive selection, fast delivery | Cluttered UI, decision fatigue, hidden fees |
| AS-2 | Niche D2C brands (e.g., Nykaa, Ajio) | Curated experience | Limited categories, higher prices |
| AS-3 | Local retail stores | See products physically | Limited inventory, no home delivery |
| AS-4 | Social commerce (Instagram shops) | Visual discovery | No structured catalogue, trust issues |
| AS-5 | Pen-and-paper shopping lists | Simple | No discovery, no pricing comparison |

**ShopEZ Differentiator:** Curated catalogue with clean brutalist UI, guest checkout (no sign-up), one-page checkout, discount visibility, and graceful offline fallback — combining the best of curated discovery and frictionless purchase.

## 9. Problem Root Cause

**What is the real reason this problem exists?**

**Root Cause:** The dominant e-commerce platforms are designed for *scale and maximization of catalogue size*, not for *user decision-making efficiency*. They compete on having the most products, resulting in information overload. Simultaneously, they force account creation early in the funnel to capture user data, introducing friction before the user has even experienced value. No established platform has yet prioritized *curated simplicity + guest-first checkout* as the core experience.

**Back story:** The rise of D2C brands proves users want curated experiences, but D2C brands are single-category. The rise of quick-commerce (Blinkit, Zepto) proves users value speed and simplicity. ShopEZ combines both insights into a multi-category curated marketplace with a checkout as fast as ordering groceries.

## 10. Your Solution

**ShopEZ — Curated E-Commerce Platform**

| Feature | How It Fits |
|---|---|
| **Curated product catalogue** grouped by category | Solves JP-1 (quick discovery) — users see quality items, not infinite feeds |
| **Guest shopping** — no sign-up required | Solves JP-2 (frictionless checkout) — users experience value before any commitment |
| **Discount badges + transparent pricing** on every product card | Solves JP-3 (trustworthy shopping) — no surprises at checkout |
| **One-page checkout** with shipping + payment | Solves JP-2 + JP-4 — minimal steps to complete purchase |
| **Order history + confirmation** | Solves JP-4 (order confidence) — users can track every order |
| **Fallback product data** when MongoDB is offline | Works within CC-4 (device/connectivity diversity) — app never shows empty state |
| **Clean cartoon-brutalist UI** with bold visuals | Works within CC-1 (time scarcity) — fast visual scanning |

### Behaviour-Fit Mapping

| Customer Behaviour | ShopEZ Feature |
|---|---|
| Searches by category (BE-1) | Category filter + grouped product display |
| Uses search bar (BE-2) | Server-side search by title, description, and category |
| Compares prices (BE-3) | Discount badges, stock indicators, clear ₹ pricing |
| Abandons cart (BE-4) | Guest cart, one-page checkout = no abandonment friction |
| Checks order history (BE-5) | Orders page with date, items, totals, payment method |
| Browses casually (BE-6) | Clean product cards with image + details in grid layout |

## Summary

The Problem-Solution Fit canvas confirms that ShopEZ addresses a real gap: **curated, fast, guest-friendly e-commerce** that contrasts with the overwhelming, account-gated experience of incumbent platforms. Every design decision — from the category-grouped catalogue to the one-page checkout to the offline fallback — maps to a specific customer behaviour, constraint, or pain point.
