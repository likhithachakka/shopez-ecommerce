## Purpose

Create a problem statement to understand your customer's point of view. The Customer Problem Statement template helps you focus on what matters to create experiences people will love. A well-articulated customer problem statement allows you and your team to find the ideal solution for the challenges your customers face.

---

## Problem Statements

| # | Problem Statement |
|---|---|
| **PS-1** | **I am** an online shopper. **I'm trying to** find quality products quickly without wading through thousands of irrelevant listings. **But** most platforms overload me with choices, slow checkouts, and unclear pricing. **Because** there is no curated, clean, and fast alternative that respects my time. **Which makes me feel** overwhelmed and distrustful — I often abandon my cart before completing a purchase. |
| **PS-2** | **I am** a small business owner or independent seller. **I'm trying to** list products and reach customers without complex setup or high costs. **But** most platforms require technical knowledge, charge high commissions, or bury my products among millions of listings. **Because** there's no simple, affordable marketplace for curated goods that gives small sellers visibility. **Which makes me feel** excluded from the online market and reliant on social media for sales. |
| **PS-3** | **I am** a busy professional. **I'm trying to** buy gifts or personal items quickly during a lunch break or commute. **But** most e-commerce sites require account creation, slow page loads, and multi-step checkout processes. **Because** they are designed for maximized session time rather than efficient purchasing. **Which makes me feel** frustrated — I often give up and buy from a local store instead. |
| **PS-4** | **I am** a first-time visitor to an online store. **I'm trying to** understand if the platform is trustworthy and whether the prices are fair. **But** hidden fees appear at checkout, discount calculations are confusing, and return policies are hard to find. **Because** the platform prioritizes capturing my data over building transparency. **Which makes me feel** anxious and skeptical — I hesitate to enter my shipping and payment details. |

## Selected Problem Statement

For the ShopEZ MVP, we have selected **PS-1** as the primary problem to solve:

> **I am** an online shopper. **I'm trying to** find quality products quickly without wading through thousands of irrelevant listings. **But** most platforms overload me with choices, slow checkouts, and unclear pricing. **Because** there is no curated, clean, and fast alternative that respects my time. **Which makes me feel** overwhelmed and distrustful — I often abandon my cart before completing a purchase.
